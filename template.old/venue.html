Airport Information

The closest airport to downtown Chicago is the Chicago Midway International Airport, but it may be more expensive than the Chicago O'Hare International Airport, depending on airline and origination.

Public transportation from both airports is easy to use.

Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW)

(Approximately 13 Miles)
Airport Shuttle Service: Shuttle service is available through GO Airport Express. Reservations are not required from Midway to downtown. Reservations are required for return trips to the airport. Shuttles depart from Midway airport every 15 minutes for downtown service to Chicago hotels. Please visit http://www.airportexpress.com/ for more information or call: 1 (888) 284-3826.
Midway shuttle fares (subject to change without notice):
Single Fare One-Way: $25.00 per person.

Taxi: Approx $35.00 - $45.00 one-way depending on time of day & traffic.

Train: From Midway Airport, take the CTA Orange Line direction towards the Loop to the Clark/Lake station. Walk east on Lake to Dearborn, turn left on Dearborn, cross the river and the Hotel Sax entrance is on the east (right) side of Dearborn. If you miss the Clark/Lake station, don't worry! Get off at State/Lake, walk west on Lake, turn right onto Dearborn, cross the river and the Hotel Sax entrance is still on the east (right) side of Dearborn. Since the train goes directly to Midway, this is the easiest and most foolproof transportation from the hotel to Midway. For additional information on the Chicago Transit Authority & fare information, please visit http://www.transitchicago.com/.

Chicago O'Hare International Airport (ORD)

(Approximately 18 miles)
Airport Shuttle Service: Shuttle service is available through GO Airport Express. Reservations are not required from O'Hare to downtown. Reservations are required for return trips to the airport. Shuttles leave O'Hare airport for downtown Chicago every 10 to 15 minutes from terminals 1E, 3F, 2D and 5E. Shuttle service begins at 4:00 a.m. for downtown hotels. Please visit http://www.airportexpress.com/ for more information or call: 1 (888) 284-3826.
O'Hare Shuttle fares (subject to change without notice):
Single Fare One-Way: $30.00 per person.

Taxi: Approx. $50.00 - $55.00 one-way depending on time of day & traffic.

Train: From O'Hare International Airport, take the CTA Blue Line towards the Loop to the Clark/Lake station. Walk east on Lake to Dearborn, turn left on Dearborn, cross the river and the Hotel Sax entrance is on the east (right) side of Dearborn. For additional information on the Chicago Transit Authority & fare information, please visit http://www.transitchicago.com/.
